Rafting Company Title

At RapidRush Expeditions, our purpose is to paddle boldly into the unknown, splashing through chaos with courage and camaraderie. Our mission is to guide adventurers through the frothy wilds with unmatched zest, waterproof wisdom, and a paddle in every hand. We live by the creed: “No current too strong, no thrill too wild,” and our motto says it all — Ride the river, find your wild.

History

RapidRush Expeditions, was founded in 1998 by three river guides who met during a stormy descent of the untamed Volta River. What began as a shared dream around a campfire — to bring the thrill of white water to anyone bold enough to try — has grown into one of the most trusted names in adventure rafting. From a single raft and a duct-taped cooler, we've expanded to serve thousands of paddlers each season, all while staying true to our roots: wild rivers, great stories, and unforgettable rides.
